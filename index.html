About Us

Sri Sai NATA Coaching is an entrance exam coaching institute in Erode which offers standardized coaching for Architecture and Design based entrance exams and is one of the leading institutes in India today. The institute focuses on creativity based courses and is offering excellent coaching for students of grade 11 and 12 who want to get into the top universities in India.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision:

To widen the scope of education by utilizing the best of all the means available to prepare an enlightened mass of citizens competent to take on the challenges posed by the modern world through intellectual, experiential, social, emotional and spiritual empowerment.

Mission:

To impart quality education that will equip industries with capable human resource to meet tomorrow’s challenges today, and fulfill the socio-economic needs of our nation by building a good rapport with industries for bridging the gap between the academia and industry To ensure everlasting quality in academic processes by constantly upgrading the methods of teaching and learning in a safe environment
